One-page promotional website proposal for a community vote on the takeover of the Moonrunners NFT collection.

Features & Requirements:

- Parallax vertical scrolling effect using assets from src/public/parallax:
    - bg.png: full-size background
    - moon.png: positioned at the top
    - clouds.png: middle layer
    - mountains.png: bottom layer
    - All images are pixelated style
- Use the Moonrunners SVG logo from the public folder
- Pixelated, retro, fantasy, mystical, and gaming-inspired design
- Brand colors: dark purples, moon/nighttime, mountains, forests, wolves
- Tone: lore-driven, succinct, meme-culture, genuine, community-focused

Sections (in order):

1. Vision (Hero Section)
    - Title: "Moonrunners Community Takeover Proposal"
    - Copy: "Our vision is to revitalize Moonrunners as an engaging and dynamic, community-driven initiative. We aim to rekindle excitement, deepen community involvement, amplify storytelling, and ensure strong continuity of the Moonrunners brand. TLDR: Protect the Pack. Make Moonrunners fun again!"
    - Visuals: Hero section with bold fonts, high contrast, and strong color usage to highlight key concepts.

2. Team
    - Two groups: "On The Hook" and "Behind The Scenes"
    - On The Hook: Biz (Cartel), Ops (Kshove)
    - Behind The Scenes: Lit (Kaladin), Fin (Dave), Dev (612), Julianne, Hodler
    - Each member: show wolf NFT image, name, and short bio/profile
    - Layout: Boxes or org chart style, fun sub-headings for each group

3. Operational
    - Checklist/audit trail for handover
    - Multisig Wallet: display address, link out, list all addresses on it (in a green box or similar)
    - Email address: display and link out (in a box)
    - Domains: list all domains to be transferred, with checkmarks for completed transfers
    - Contracts: list each ETH contract (collection, description, # of items, current owner)
    - Discord: list name/ID and current owner
    - Twitter: list name/ID and current follows
    - Visuals: Professional, technical, official documentation style

4. Communication
    - Commitments:
        - Daily greetings (gms)
        - Weekly Discord communication
        - Monthly community virtual events
        - Quarterly business updates
    - Visuals: Schedule or timeline, use icons/emojis for engagement
    - Copy: Emphasize transparency, frequent updates, community participation, and staying true to Moonrunners' ethos

5. What's Next
    - Briefly mention that there are many ideas for the future, but avoid overcommitting
    - Visuals: Simple, forward-looking

6. Call To Action
    - Encourage community participation in the vote and engagement with the proposal
    - Visuals: Prominent, clear, and motivating

All requirements above should be implemented in a single, visually engaging, scrollable web page. Use assets and branding as specified. Each section should be clearly separated and visually distinct, following the overall brand and tone guidelines.
