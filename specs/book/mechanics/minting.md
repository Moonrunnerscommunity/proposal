# Minting Mechanics

## What Moonspeakers Do

They grant ACCESS, not levels.

A Moonspeaker Mints a wolf into the Ledger. That wolf starts at Level 1. Everything after is earned.

**Why this matters:** The Minted claim the Ledger is birthright. Moonspeakers prove it's a lie.

---

## Minting Prerequisites

### Moon Cycles

| Moon | Capacity |
|------|----------|
| New Moon | None |
| Half Moon | 1-3 wolves |
| Full Moon | 7-12 wolves |
| Blood Moon | 30-50 wolves (yearly) |
| Convergence | Unknown (theoretical) |

### Wolf Requirements

**Pack Witness:** Seven wolves vouch with their lives.

**The Trial:** Candidate proves worthiness.

**The Oath:** Candidate declares purpose. Becomes binding.

### Moonspeaker Requirements

| Present | Multiplier |
|---------|------------|
| 1 | Base |
| 2 | 3x |
| 3+ | Exponential |
| All 6 | Unknown |

### The Cost

**Fatigue:** Too many per cycle = collapse.

**Bond Strain:** More wolves = thinner connection.

**The Bleed:** High-volume Minting changes you.
