# Timeline

```
YEAR 0: The Mint
└── 10,000 wolves connect to the Ledger

YEARS 0-100: The Moonspeaker Era
├── Original six Moonspeakers maintain balance
├── They Mint sparingly — access is a gift, not a right
└── Tension builds between Minted and Unminted

YEAR ~100: The Betrayal
├── Andarax (Moonspeaker) turns on the others
├── The Minted use this to justify killing all Moonspeakers
└── "They were corrupt. We had no choice."

YEARS 100-900: The Long Silence
├── Ledger access becomes hereditary
├── The Minted consolidate into aristocracy
├── The Unminted become permanent underclass
└── History rewritten — Moonspeakers were villains

YEAR ~900: The First Summoning
├── Desperate Unminted attempt to bring back Moonspeakers
├── Something goes wrong (MYSTERY BOX)
└── The Minted crack down hard

YEAR 1000: The Second Summoning (NOW)
├── A century of secret preparation
├── Six NFT holders pulled through
└── The revolution begins
```
