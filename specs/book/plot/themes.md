# Themes

## Primary

- **Taking the Leap** — What happens when you finally say yes to the thing that scares you?
- **Earned vs. Inherited** — Power means nothing if you didn't earn it
- **Building from Nothing** — The startup nobody believed in vs. the empire that's always existed
- **NPC Revolution** — The background characters demand to be protagonists

## Secondary

- Found family under pressure
- The cost of playing it safe
- Who writes history?
- When does "fake it till you make it" become real?
